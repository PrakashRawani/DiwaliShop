About
The Festival of Lights

Diwali, also known as Divali or Deepawali, is a major festival celebrated over five days in many parts of India by people of different faiths . Many Hindus observe Diwali by lighting small oil lamps known as ‘diyas’ in honor of Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth and good fortune. The lamps symbolize the triumph of light over darkness and evil.
